Kenji's Top 5 games

Welcome to Kenjis Top 5 games of all time

Theres a lot of game that have come out over the years but there is 10 games that take the icing on the cake of most influential, fun, and interactive games to ever exist

#5 Fortnite

By Epic games

Fortnite is Battle royale where you fight 99 other people for the Victory Royale where only one stands tall.

#4 FNaF (five nights at freddys)

By Scott Cawthon

FNaF also know as Five Nights at Freddys is a scary horror game where you work in a pizza place at night and try to survive while big scary animatronics attack you.

#3 Minecraft

By Notch

Minecraft is an open world where the player is the only thing that stops the ever ending imagination.

#2 Sonic Adventure 2

By Sega

Sonic Adventure 2 or SA2 for short is a high speed Platformer where you control mutiple characters but your MC is Sonic the hedgehog.

#1 PAC-MAN

By Namco Bandai

PAC-MAN is a very influential game it was one of the most popular arcade games to ever exist it was a quick pace game where you have to get every pellet while avoiding the ghosts.
