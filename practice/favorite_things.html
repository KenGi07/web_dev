Ramen

Ramen is a silky smooth noodle soup that has the flavor of pork and more. The egg and the silkiness melts in your mouth. There is multiple flavors like miso, tonkotsu, and more i will be rating 3 flavors and will explain what they taste like.

  1. tonkotsu
  2. shoyu
  3. miso

Miso ramen is a silky more salty flavor and they use different noodles they use egg noodles instead of wheat, tonkotsu has that pork flavor and fattyness they use tradional wheat noodles, and shoyu is just both combined but with egg noodles
