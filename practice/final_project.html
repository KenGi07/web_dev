Zion National Park

Zion National Park is a beatiful region of rocks and hiking where you can see the beauty of south Utah

Zion has great scenery and multiple great hiking areas that you can go to. Gaze up at massive sandstone cliffs of cream, pink, and red that soar into a brilliant blue sky. Experience the tall, stretching walls of a narrow slot canyon. Zions unique array of plants and animals will enchant you as you absorb the rich history of the past and enjoy the excitement of present day adventures.

  • Beautiful scenery
  • Great hiking spots
  • Good food
  • Very fast to enter and open in the winter
